EyeSpy Support

Need help?

This page exists so App Store users have actual contact details instead of falling into support purgatory.

kai@eyespy.care Privacy Policy

What to include in your email

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • What screen you were on when the problem happened.
  • Whether the issue involves purchases, reminders, widget status, or clinic linking.
  • A screenshot if the bug is visible.

Fast answers

  • Notifications not showing up? Check iOS Settings → Notifications → EyeSpy and confirm alerts are allowed.
  • Wrong clinic linked? Open EyeSpy → Settings → Practice Link, then change or clear the clinic link.
  • Purchase not showing? Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchase.
  • Widget looks stale? Open the app once, update your session, and the widget should refresh.

Response channel

Support and product feedback go to kai@eyespy.care. That is the working support contact for launch.
